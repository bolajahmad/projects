### [PolkaBill Protocol](https://github.com/bolajahmad/polkabill-protocol) - A distributed stablecoin-based subscription and payments protocol for EVM wallets

A cross-chain recurring payment system that uses stablecoins billing for digital services. It allows
merchants to define subscrption plans and collect payment across their services without relying on any
centralized product other than their wallets.

- Defined a secure payment protocol that compliments every other available tool and can truly be owned by Users.
- Removed the latency problem that affects cross-chain applications by building a resilient payment protocol on-chain.
- Wrote gas-optimized and secure Solidity smart contracts that ensure Merchants get settled and Subscribers can easily prove their payment.
- Exposed Typescript SDKs for merchants to integrate into app, and also dashboard for Users to manage subscriptions.

<small>
  *Skills: Solidity, Wallets, Cross-chain, Solidity, Architecture, React*
</small>

### [Decentralized Agentic Payments](https://github.com/bolajahmad/decentralized-agentic-payment) - machine-native payment infrastructure

that enables AI agents to autonomously consume APIs and compute services under
user-controlled authorization.

- Wrote ERC-4735 based smart contracts to allow session-based payments by machines for digital services.
- Improved security and efficiency of x402 payents by implement proof-based batched payment approaches.
- Exposed JS-based CLI tools and SDKs for allowing EOA to monitor activities of agents (or machines) spendign their funds.
- Integrated application with a live agent (like CLaude desktop) to trigger payment using chat and code options.

<small>
  *Skills: EVM, Wallets, x402, MCP, Architecture, Express, Goldsky/TheGraph*
</small>
