# Professional Summary

Rust engineer transitioning into blockchain protocol and distributed systems
engineering.

Over the past several years I have built blockchain applications while
progressively moving closer to protocol-level development through Bitcoin,
CKB and Polkadot ecosystems.

My recent work centers around understanding blockchain internals including
transaction validation, scripting systems, runtime execution, cryptographic
primitives, networking, wallet infrastructure and distributed protocol design.
Alongside open source contributions, I regularly document my research,
implement protocol experiments and build developer tooling to deepen my
understanding of blockchain infrastructure.
